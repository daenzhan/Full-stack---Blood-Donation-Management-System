Donation Conditions
Before you donate blood, please ensure:
Basic Requirements
  • You are between 18 and 65 years old
  • Your weight is at least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • You are in good general health
  • You have valid identification
Health Conditions
  • No fever, cold, or flu symptoms
  • No recent surgeries (within 6 months)
  • No new tattoos or piercings (within 4 months)
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding
Preparation
  • Have a good meal 2-3 hours before donation
  • Drink plenty of fluids
  • Avoid fatty foods before donation
  • Get adequate sleep the night before
Time Between Donations
  • Whole blood: 56 days (8 weeks)
  • Plasma: 28 days (4 weeks)
  • Platelets: 7 days (1 week)
Important: Please consult with medical staff if you have any questions about your eligibility to donate.
